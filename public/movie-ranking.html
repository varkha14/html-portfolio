The Best Movies according to Vaishnavi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

Loved the story between Kabir and Naina. Contains the most beautiful songs in it.

Taare Zameen Par

The story of Ishaan who suffers from dyslexia and his art teacher, Ram who help him overcome it is just amazing.

Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!

The 90's best film features the love story between Prem and Nisha with a family drama.
